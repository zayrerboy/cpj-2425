To introduce the blurred lines between the natural and unnatural, as suggested by the concept of queer ecology, I looked for pop-culutral references to explain this concept to the atelier.

The concept of queer ecology is future-oriented. It's fictional and questions the reality through intimate connection with nature. Immediately I thought of go-to reference 'Bob's Burgers'. The episode of Bob and the scared cow where he questioned his faith for Linda. Next, I had to pull out the futuristic and vulgar 'Rick and Morty'. The critically acclaimed love story of a merman and a woman and finally, the post-apocalytpic world of hybrids.
