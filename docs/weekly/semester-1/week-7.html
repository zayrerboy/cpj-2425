‚ë° Task Two: What is Interspecies??! üîé

The term interspecies became increasingly ambiguous as I read papers across technology, design and ecology from scholars referring to words like human and non-human interacting with each other in different contexts.

As a visual-learner, this exercise aims to apply concept into practice through personal images. The objective of this task was to seek clarity and untangle the web of newfound knowledge.

      Reflection:

    • The result from this task was realising interspecies is indeed so fluid. What Donna refers to interspecies is the act of human interacting with a non-human aiming for mutual benefits through technology. Every relationship between species can be studied from an ecological standpoint to ultimately minimise human-biases. So, what my research focus on is a future where hybrid entities are attached to environment sensors to encourage environmental stewardship. In this sense, environmental sensors are the technology in question.
