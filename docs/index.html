Dreaming of an Interspecies Sensor

We live in a reality where electronic devices can empower us to perceive the environment beyond our physical body. But how can we reconnect with the physical environment in this digital age when technology are constantly employed to optimise everyday self-survillience? Dreaming of an Interspecies Sensor aims to explore this question by leveraging on humans' innate biophillia and anthropomorphism, reimgaining human/nature interactions to foster a connection with the environment. It aims to ask if sensor technology enable or disable our connection with the environment.
